| Introduction on standards system of the pharmaceutical packaging materials in the Chinese Pharmacopoeia 2025 Edition | Pharmaceutical packaging materials is an integral part of drugs, and its standards system is also an important part of the Chinese Pharmacopoeia. According to the Notice of the Provisions on the Primary Responsibilities, Internal Organizational Structure and Staffing of the Affiliated Institution under National Medical Products Administration (GYJR [2018] No. 60), the Chinese Pharmacopoeia Commission (ChPC) undertakes the technical review, naming of generic names, interpretation and technical consultation of national standards of pharmaceutical excipients and pharmaceutical packaging materials. According to the responsibility requirements, aiming to make the overall planning of the national pharmaceutical packaging materials standards system, Chinese Pharmacopoeia Commission and its specialized committee of pharmaceutical packaging materials have organized the construction of the national pharmaceutical packaging materials standards system.<br>On the basis of first inclusion for pharmaceutical packaging materials standards system in the Chinese Pharmacopoeia 2025 Edition, which can contribute to accurately understand and utilize the standards in the Chinese Pharmacopoeia.<br>1 Working background of the standards system construction of the pharmaceutical packaging materials in the Chinese Pharmacopoeia<br>1.1 Main development stage of China's national pharmaceutical packaging materials standards<br>China's national standards of pharmaceutical packaging materials are closely related to the development of pharmaceutical industry and the transformation of supervision pattern. By industry and supervision development, the national standards of pharmaceutical packaging materials can be broadly divided into three stages. <br>The first stage is the beginning of the pharmaceutical industry - early stage of pharmaceutical packaging materials standard where management gradually moving to legalization and standardization. From the founding of People's Republic of China to the 1990s, the variety of pharmaceutical packaging materials in China is limited., and the pharmaceutical packaging materials standards implemented were mainly GB and YY standards.<br>The second stage is the rapid development of the pharmaceutical industry - the YBB standards stage under the registration and approval system. In order to meet the needs of the rapid development of the pharmaceutical industry and adapt to the implementation of the administrative registration and approval system for pharmaceutical packaging materials, former China Food and Drug Administration successively issued 6 volumes of national pharmaceutical packaging materials standards (YBB standards) from 2002 to 2006. Subsequently, the National Institutes for Food and Drug Control, as the leading unit, organized the sorting, correction and compilation of 6 volumes of YBB standards (hereinafter referred to as the YBB standards 2015 Edition).<br>The third stage is the high-quality development of the pharmaceutical industry - the exploring stage of the new national pharmaceutical packaging materials standards system under the bundling review system. Since 2015, pharmaceutical RD and regulatory authorities have become increasingly aware of the importance of pharmaceutical packaging materials quality management, bundling review with preparations and national pharmaceutical packaging materials standards. The pharmaceutical packaging materials standards are being constantly added in the Chinese Pharmacopoeia, paving the way for the transformation and upgrading of national pharmaceutical packaging materials standards.<br>1.2 Role and problems of YBB standards 2015 Edition<br>In the registration management stage of pharmaceutical packaging materials, 5807 kinds of pharmaceutical packaging materials were granted the approval number. The YBB standards cover the main variety of pharmaceutical packaging materials actually used in drug production. Over the years, the YBB standards have played an important role in guiding the production and quality control of pharmaceutical packaging materials enterprises, the capacity building of inspection institution, and the quality supervision of pharmaceutical packaging materials, providing convenience for industry technicians, reviewers and regulators, and have been widely used in the industry. However, with further promotion of the bundling review of excipient and packaging materials with preparations, the promulgation and implementation of the new Drug Administration Law and Measures for the Administration of Drug Standards, the continuous strengthening of risk management, whole process control and key responsibility of enterprises, and the further integration of China's standard setting concept into the international standards after China joining ICH, the problem of disharmony between the YBB standards 15 Edition and enterprise needs and regulatory requirements has gradually become prominent: first, part of the standard content does not adapt to the industry development under the bundling review system; second, there is a large gap in basic and general standards; third, part of the standard content lags behind the development of analytical technology. <br>1.3 Inclusion of pharmaceutical packaging materials standards in foreign pharmacopoeias<br>Relevant standards for pharmaceutical packaging materials have been included in United States Pharmacopoeia, European Pharmacopoeia and Japanese Pharmacopoeia as well as pharmacopoeias of other countries. Considering the diversity and compatibility of the use of pharmaceutical packaging materials, the standard form of general requirements with materials and their containers as the main line is mainly adopted, rather than the monographs standard form commonly used in drugs. Although the United States Pharmacopoeia, European Pharmacopoeia and Japanese Pharmacopoeia have been internationally harmonized for many years, the relevant harmonization work in the field of pharmaceutical packaging materials standards has not been carried out, and there is still a certain degree of difference among them. The pharmaceutical packaging materials standards continue to be developed or revised in the pharmacopoeias according to national regulations, policies and actual use in various countries.<br>In general, the pharmaceutical packaging materials standards system in the United States Pharmacopoeia is relatively complete. A specific expert committee has been established for the preparation and revision of the relevant standards for pharmaceutical packaging materials, so that the pharmaceutical packaging materials standards system can reflect the actual needs of the pharmaceutical industry quickly, and be continuously developed and improved. Standard types include general chapters, monograph standards, method standards, guidelines, etc., and different types of standards support each other. The pharmaceutical packaging materials standards in the European Pharmacopoeia only involve some monographs, and the relevant guidance content for production and use is scattered in the standards for pharmaceutical packaging materials and the general chapters of preparations, and has not yet formed a more complete standards system. However, as far as the standards for plastic materials are concerned, they are set relatively reasonably, basically covering the main plastic materials for pharmaceutical packaging, and have been referenced and adopted in the United States Pharmacopoeia because of their high operability. Few pharmaceutical packaging materials standards are included in the Japanese Pharmacopoeia, mainly involving packaging containers for injections, and have not formed a relatively complete standards system. <br>2 Overall idea of the construction of the pharmaceutical packaging materials standards system in the Chinese Pharmacopoeia 2025 Edition<br>After preliminary research and evaluation, it is confirmed that if the one standard for one packaging material" pattern of the original YBB standards is maintained, it is not only required to increase the number of monograph standards in the national pharmaceutical packaging materials standards system, but also required to modify the general testing methods, and change the relatively fixed inspection rules and limits in the original provisions. The revised content is entensive and complex, do like this can neither meet the needs of industry development and regulatory requirements, nor comply with the development trend of relevant international advanced standards. Chinese Pharmacopoeia Commission has set up a special research topic and entrusted Shanghai Institute for Food and Drug Packaging Materials Testing to lead the Research on the Planning and Style Specification of Pharmaceutical Packaging Materials Standards System, in order to fully learn from the advanced experience of foreign pharmacopoeia standards and relevant industry standards, and build a pharmaceutical packaging materials standards system suitable for China's industry development and regulatory needs.<br>The overall idea of the construction of the pharmaceutical packaging materials standards in the new edition of pharmacopoeia is to sort out and follow up the standards system and development trend of domestic and foreign pharmaceutical packaging materials and related fields, fully learn from and absorb advanced concepts and superior technologies, pay attention to the connection and complementarity with relevant regulations and technical documents based on risk management and whole-process management for protection of drug safety and effectiveness, cooperate in the bundling review, make top-level design of Chinas pharmaceutical packaging materials standards system, guide and urge drug manufacturers to select pharmaceutical packaging materials that meet the safety and health standards, ensure the quality of drugs, and meet the requirements of drug storage and transportation and medical use.<br>3 Working process of pharmaceutical packaging materials standards system in the Chinese Pharmacopoeia 2025 Edition<br>In the drafting process of pharmaceutical packaging materials standards in the new edition of pharmacopoeia, Chinese Pharmacopoeia Commission and each work group organized more than 100 meetings. The draft standards have been publicized several times, and all the publicized drafts are accompanied with drafting instructions to facilitate understanding and grasp by all parties; the drafts for publicity of some standards are published in English at the same time, which is convenient for domestic and foreign drug and pharmaceutical packaging materials enterprises to research and verify. The construction of the national pharmaceutical packaging materials standards system is characterized by "tight time, heavy task and great influence; less experience, less funding, less manpower ". In the preliminary work, Chinese Pharmacopoeia Commission actively practiced the standard formation mechanism as government-led, enterprise-dominated and community-involved.<br>3.1 Give full play to all parties<br>To ensure the orderly promotion of the construction of the pharmaceutical packaging materials standards system, it is import to give full play to the advantages of all parties, and implement organization and coordination. First, Chinese Pharmacopoeia Commission has publicly solicited volunteer units joining in the construction of the national pharmaceutical packaging materials standards system through the website, and a total of 39 inspection institution and 29 related enterprises have filled in the letter of intent. Second, based on the original work foundation and standard research capabilities, about 50 inspection institution and associations have been recruited to form a core working group. Third, special working groups are formed one by one, the division of responsibilities of relevant units and personnel is defined, and the designated main review projects of each commission member are determined. Fourth, the special working groups absorb relevant units to participate in the standard research according to the work needs. Fifth, with the help of the technical guidance role for the national inspection institution, the National Institutes for Food and Drug Control is entrusted to organize more than 40 inspection institution to carry out expanded validation on the proposed general testing methods, to further ensure the operability and rigor of the standards, and urge provincial inspection institution to reserve the inspection capabilities and hardware conditions. Sixth, play the role of a bridge between industry organizations, and build two-way communication channels between pharmaceutical packaging materials enterprises and standard makers through associations such as China National Pharmaceutical Packaging Association and China Pharmaceutical Association of Plant Engineering. Seventh, set up a pilot in Shanghai and establish cooperation between Shanghai Institute for Food and Drug Packaging Materials Testing and Shanghai Center for Drug Evaluation and Inspection to study the standardization of standard changes after the implementation of the new standards system. Eighth, regulators, review departments, inspection departments, sampling departments, etc. are invited to participate in the discussion and fully listen to the commitment of all parties.<br>3.2 Establish a standard coordination mechanism<br>Based on the cooperation concept of mutual trust, equality and pragmatism, the Sino-US Pharmacopoeia working group on metal pharmaceutical packaging materials is formed to jointly promote the research of relevant standards. Meanwhile, a two-way coordination mechanism between national standards and group standards is established. For the research project organized by the Chinese Pharmacopoeia Commission in the early stage, if it is not suitable included in the national standards now, it will be promoted as group standard first by the association has been entrusted; the group standard organized by the association in the early stage, if it is suitable included in the national standards, it has been considered for inclusion in the national standards system.<br>3.3 Expand the feedback collection from all parties<br>Since the new standards system have changed greatly with the existing standards from concept to form, a series of measures have been taken as follows to ensure the fast promotion and smooth transition of the new standards system. First, grassroots research and discussion. The ChPC leaders lead the team to conduct research and discussion at the grassroots level and in the enterprises to understand the work progress and existing problems. Second, make an evaluation report. Shandong Institute of Medical Device and Pharmaceutical Packaging Inspection is entrusted to carry out the evaluation and investigation of the national pharmaceutical packaging material standards system in Shandong Province, the investigate scope covered the representative units of pharmaceutical packaging materials production and use in Shandong Province, and make an investigation report aiming to make timely adjustment and improvement to the preparation and revision of the standards. Third, carry out publicity and training. The special training and publicity on the pharmaceutical packaging materials standards have been carried out more than 10 times, cumulatively involving nearly 10,000 participants.<br>4 Main framework of pharmaceutical packaging materials standards system in the Chinese Pharmacopoeia 2025 Edition<br>The pharmaceutical packaging materials standards system in the new edition of Chinese Pharmacopoeia, including 1+4+58 standards, can be divided into two parts: "main body framework", comprising the general requirements of the pharmaceutical packaging materials and the four materials of glass, rubber, plastic and metal; second, support system, comprising a series of general testing methods for pharmaceutical packaging materials.<br>4.1 Main body framework<br>1 is 9621 Guidelines for General Requirements for Drug Packaging Materials and Containers, which is the most top-level standard of the pharmaceutical packaging materials standard systems in the Chinese Pharmacopoeia. During the revision process of the new edition of pharmacopoeia, the Drug Administration Law, domestic and foreign pharmacopoeia standards and related technical documents were compared and analyzed, focusing on the pharmaceutical packaging materials that are most closely related to the guarantee of drug quality from the concept of pharmaceutical packaging system, and the general requirements and overall requirements for the quality control of pharmaceutical packaging materials were put forward from the perspective of whole process management and risk control. The full text includes the definition, classification, naming, four characteristics (protection, compatibility, safety and functionality) and self-stability requirements, production requirements, use requirements and inspection rules of pharmaceutical packaging materials.<br>4 is the guidelines for the four most important materials in the pharmaceutical packaging - glass, rubber closure, plastic and metal, and it is the backbone of the pharmaceutical packaging materials standards system in the Chinese Pharmacopoeia. Edition<br>5.1 Improve the overall level of China's national pharmaceutical packaging materials standards system<br>First, in view of the wide variety of materials, shapes and uses of pharmaceutical packaging materials, the standards are included in the new edition of pharmacopoeia in the form of "1+4+58", which is convenient to integrate with the international pharmaceutical packaging materials Second, although the standards for metal pharmaceutical packaging materials have not been included in the pharmacopoeia of all countries at present, in view of China's standard research capabilities, the United States Pharmacopoeia Convention took the initiative to set up a joint working group on metal pharmaceutical packaging materials standards with Chinese Pharmacopoeia Commission to study collectively and form their own draft standards.<br>5.2 Promote the implementation of key responsibilities of enterprises<br>The original YBB standards are monographs, and the test items, methods and limits wherein are applied in a certain scope. The standards are included in the new edition of pharmacopoeia in the form of 1+4+58, so that the pharmaceutical packaging materials standard system in China is both rigid and malleable, which is conducive to promoting enterprises' in-depth knowledge and understanding of products and standards, and promoting enterprises to fully implement their key responsibilities.<br>5.3 Provide more effective technical support for bundling review<br>At present, there are only more than 80 monographs in YBB standards, far from 15,000 registration numbers. The new edition of pharmacopoeia is divided by four major materials and specific variety to reflect the key points that should be investigated in the production, selection and quality control of various pharmaceutical packaging materials, and provide a more comprehensive and intuitive technical reference for drug reviewers.<br>5.4 Promote the industry-wide improvement on the inspection capacity of pharmaceutical packaging materials <br>A total of 58 general testing methods for pharmaceutical packaging materials are included in the new edition of pharmacopoeia. This standards are provided with separate standard numbers to facilitate the certification and accreditation of laboratory competence by inspection and testing institution, and facilitate the formulation of enterprise standards according to national standards, so as to effectively standardize the industry-wide inspection operation of pharmaceutical packaging materials and improve the quality control level of pharmaceutical packaging materials of government inspection institution and enterprises.<br>To address timely all parties' consultation on the pharmaceutical packaging materials standards in the Chinese Pharmacopoeia , Chinese Pharmacopoeia Commission issued a notice on the implementation of the guardianship management of the pharmaceutical packaging materials standards of monographs in the Chinese Pharmacopoeia in 2021, and published the contact information of the guardian of the pharmaceutical packaging materials standards in the second edition of Chinese Pharmacopoeia in January 2025.
