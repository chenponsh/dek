| Introduction of the main addition and revision of the Chinese Pharmacopoeia 2025 Edition (Volume II) | The Chinese Pharmacopoeia 2025 Edition will be promulgated and implemented. It consists of four volumes, of which Volume II mainly includes chemical drugs. Over the past five years, the Chinese Pharmacopoeia Commission (ChPC) has always adhered to the four strictest instructions of the General Secretary Xi Jinping and has carefully organized the relevant institutions and expert committees to complete the compilation of the Volume II of Chinese Pharmacopoeia 2025 Edition focusing on the goal of establishing the most rigorous standards, adhering to the people-centered development thought and focusing on the public drug safety and efficacy. This article introduces the main characteristics, additions and revisions of this volume of pharmacopoeia to facilitate the users of Chinese Pharmacopoeia to better interpret and apply to this volume. <br>1 The selection of monographs is based on more scientific and rigorous principle<br>Only the drugs which are clinically commonly used with accurate efficacy, safe for use, stable quality and perfect quality control standards can be included in the Chinese Pharmacopoeia 2025 Edition (Volume II). The monographs to be included in the new edition of the pharmacopoeia need to be approved by the expert committee of pharmaceutical practitioner and the expert committee of medical practitioner. During the process of selection the principle of the high quality of drug standards and the high requirements for the clinical accessibility is followed. On the one hand, drug standards are improved scientifically and reasonably based on the program of the improvement of national drug standards, combined with the research outcomes of the National Drug Sampling and Testing program (hereinafter referred to as Sampling and Testing program), generic drug quality and efficacy equivalency evaluation (hereinafter referred to as equivalency evaluation) and other research outcomes. On the other hand, considering actural clinical needs, attention is paid to the update of the Catalogue of National Essential Medicines (hereinafter referred to as the Essential Medicines Catalogue) and the Catalogue of Medicines for National Basic Medical Insurance, Industrial Injury Insurance and Maternity Insurance (hereinafter referred to as the Medical Insurance Catalogue). The new monographs in the Chinese Pharmacopoeia 2025 Edition (Volume II) cover antineoplastic drugs (e.g. Hydroxycarbamide capsules, Arsenic Trioxide for Injection, etc.), cardiovascular medications (Candesartan Cilexetil and Hydrochlorothiazide Tablets (I) and (II), etc.), hypoglycemic agents (Mitiglinide Calcium Tablets), antibacterials (Moxifloxacin Hydrochloride Tablets, etc.), endocrine drugs (Bicalutamide Tablets), anticoagulants (Indobufen Tablets, Dalteparin Sodium Injection, etc.), systemic antihistamines (Desloratadine Tablets), cough and cold preparations (Fudosteine Tablets, etc.), corticosteroids (Mometasone Furoate Cream, etc.), parenteral nutrition preparations (Medium and Long Chain Fat Emulsion Injection) and other chemical drugs in the Essential Medicines Catalogue (2018 Edition) and the Medical Insurance Catalogue (2023 edition) , further meeting the needs of the public for medications. For example: (1) Over the years, more attentions have been paid on the quality and specifications of low molecular heparin products, which are widely used in clinical practice. Therefore, in the process of the program of improving national drug standards, the domestic and foreign drug standards of the above preparations have been compared in detail, and thoroughly studied and confirmed based on the product characteristics. In view of the quality issues found in the Sampling and Testing program, the drug standards have been further improved, and finally the monographs of Dalteparin Sodium Injection, Enoxaparin Sodium Injection and Nadroparin Calcium Injection have been included in the new edition of pharmacopoeia after approval by the expert committee of pharmaceutical practitioner and the expert committee of medical practitioner. (2) In recent years, the research and development of anti-tumor drugs in China has made major breakthroughs both in the field of fromcytotoxic drugs to small molecule targeted drugs, and in the field of from immunotherapy drugs to cell therapy drugs, so that greatly meet the needs of cancer patients for medications. The Chinese Pharmacopoeia 2025 Edition (Volume II) focuses on the development of anti-tumor drugs in China, sticks to the principle of clinical need-oriented, continues to strengthen the development and revision of the standards of anti-tumor drugs commonly used in clinical practice relying on the national action plan for improving drug standards, and includes a series of anti-tumor drug with accurate efficacy and mature standards, including the anti-tumor drugs of Arsenic Trioxide for Injection and Lobaplatin for Injection independently developed by China.<br>The Chinese Pharmacopoeia 2025 Edition (Volume II) includes a total of 2776 monographs, including 66 new monographs. In addition, the monographs of Purified Water and Water for Injection have been moved to the Chinese Pharmacopoeia 2025 Edition (Volume IV), and the monograph of Sterile Water for Injection remains in Volume II.<br>2 The General Notices becomes more uniform and guiding<br>General Notices is an important part of the Chinese Pharmacopoeia, presents the basic principles for the proper interpretation and application of the Chinese Pharmacopoeia, provides the uniform regulations on the common issues in individual monographs, general requirements and general statements related to quality control of drugs, and serves as the baton of the Chinese Pharmacopoeia. It provides the basis for resolution of any dispute in drug quality testing, ensures the fairness of the testing results and help users for the proper interpretation and application of the pharmacopoeia. The General Notices of the Chinese Pharmacopoeia 2025 Edition (Volume II) has its own characteristics of Volume II on the basis of harmonization of general requirements for all the four Volumes . The main additions and revisions are as follows.<br>2.1 In line with the provisions of the general requirements in the Measures for the Administration of Drug Standards , in Article 2, guidelines are not as part of the general requirements any more and state that the guidelines are recommendations, not mandatory.<br>2.2 Add the word CAS number in Article 9 in line with the changes in the text of monographs.<br>2.3 Add requirements for risk control of residual solvents, elemental impurities and genotoxic impurities in Article 17. General Chapter 0861 Residual Solvents, General Chapter 0862 Elemental Impurities and Guideline 9306 Genotoxic Impurities have been hoarmonized with ICH related guidlines. The Chinese Pharmacopoeia (Volume II) adheres to the strategy of gradual implementation and steady transition based on Chinas national conditions, properly promotes the transformation and implementation of ICH Q3C and ICH Q3D guidelines in Volume II and continues to pay attention to the safety control of genotoxic impurities. Article 17 encourages the drug Marketing Authorization Holder (MAH) to use the concept and method of risk management to ？ identify, evaluate and control of the risk of residual solvents and elemental impurities. The MAH can submit an assessment report to the drug regulatory authority of the State Council after completing the relevant risk assessment of the product, and apply for the corresponding revision of the drug standard for the product. And ？ to conduct risk assessment of genotoxic impurities, and develop reasonable testing methods if necessary. The limits shall meet the requirements of Guideline 9306 Genotoxic Impurities. It should be noted that in Guideline 9306 Genotoxic Impurities, it clearly states that the content is derived from relevant ICH guidelines and if the content of ICH guidelines changes or inconsistent with the Guideline 9306, the latest edition of ICH Guidelines can be referred to.<br>2.4 In Article 21, the sentence The packaging containers of injections and ophthalmic preparations directly contact the drug, which are regarded as part of the preparations. well closed is stated under the item of Storage. Drug storage conditions are closely related to the nature of a drug and its dosage form. With the continuous innovation and development of drugs in China, this part cannot accurately describe the storage requirements of related preparations.<br>2.5 In Article 25, it is further clarifies that the significant digits rounding should follow the rounding rulesdescribed in GB/T8170-2008 .<br>3 The standard system of chemical drugs<br>3.1 The adopted names of chemical drugs <br>It is clearly stipulated in the Drug Administration Law of the People's Republic of China that A drug name listed in the national drug standards is an adopted name in China, and it is stipulated in the Provisions for Drug Registration that Chinese Pharmacopoeia Commission (hereinafter referred to as ChPC) shall undertake adopted name approval. Based on the international nonproprietary names (INN) system, ChPC has formulated the Chinese Approved Drug Names system to maintain the science, consistency and fairness of the adopted names in China. ？ The adopted name that may be misused have been standardized in the new edition of pharmacopoeia, such as Lyophilized Thrombin Powder included in the Chinese Pharmacopoeia 2020 Edition that can be used for external or oral use. The product with the word lyophilized is easy to be misunderstood as an injection, so the name is revised as the Thrombin Powder in the new edition of pharmacopoeia. ？ The titles of new monographs have been standardized according to the naming principle of the adopted name. An example is compound preparation of candesartan cilexetil and hydrochlorothiazide. The adopted name of the marketed product is Candesartan Cilexetil and Hydrochlorothiazide Tablets. They have the same composition but different prescription ratio, so the adopted name in the new edition of pharmacopoeia is specified as Candesartan Cilexetil and Hydrochlorothiazide tablets (I) and Candesartan Cilexetil and Hydrochlorothiazide Tablets (II). l The titles of new monographs have been standardized according to the general chapters of preparations. An example is Terbutaline Sulfate Solution for Inhalation. The adopted name of the marketed product is Terbutaline Sulfate Nebuliser Solution. According to the General Chapter 0111 Preparations for Inhalation in the Chinese Pharmacopoeia (Volume IV), liquid preparations for inhalation refer to the liquid preparations for the nebulizer, so it is not necessary to have nebuliser in the adopted name. Meanwhile, the solution for inhalation is specified as a concentrated solution that needs to be diluted before use. However, this product does not need to be diluted before use, so the adopted name in the new edition of pharmacopoeia is specified as Terbutaline Sulfate Solution for Inhalation.<br>For the drugs with the adopted names revised in the new edition of pharmacopoeia, the original names are specified as former names during the transition period.<br>3.2 The basic drug information becomes more accurate and practical<br>3.2.1 Reconfirmation of molecular weight. Atomic weight is the basis of molecular weight calculation and the basis for accurately obtaining the relative mass of compounds. The Table of Standard Atomic Weights in the Chinese Pharmacopoeia 2025 Edition (Volume IV) has been updated according to the 2021 edition Table of Standard Atomic Weights of the International Union of Pure and Applied Chemistry (IUPAC). Therefore, the molecular weights of nearly 1870 drugs and impurities involved in the Chinese Pharmacopoeia 2025 Edition (Volume II) have been recalculated, and about 700 molecular weights have been finally updated, ensuring the accuracy and timeliness of drug standards. Meanwhile, the titer changed with the molecular weight has also been confirmed and updated.<br>3.2.2 Addition of CAS number. Each chemical substance has a unique CAS number, and the addition of CAS number to the new edition of pharmacopoeia standard helps the user quickly and accurately identify drug ingredients and improve the efficacy of drug administration. After the checking and confirmation of the relevant information on 1269 monographs in the new edition of pharmacopoeia, it is finally determined to add about 940 CAS numbers, further improving the standardization and practicability of the pharmacopoeial standards. Global universality of the CAS numbers (CAS numbers are included in the United States Pharmacopeia, European Pharmacopoeia and other foreign main pharmacopoeias) can better promote the mutual recognition of drug standards in China and other countries, and following the unified standards will help the recognition of Chinas drugs in the international market while enhancing international competitiveness.<br>3.2.3 Appearance and solubility. The General Notices stipulates that the appearance and solubility of the drug are used as reference information. (1) The pharmacopoeial standards put forward common requirements for the appearance of the same drug, and do not describe the personalized content of individual enterprise products. For example, for Sildenafil Citrate Tablets, the color of the film coating is not specified, but only the color after removal of the coating is specified. (2) There are many factors that affect the solubility of drug substances, and are not suitable to be spcified in the pharmacopoeial standards as the unified results cannot be obtained after experiments. For example, it is proved by experiments that fudosteine has different degrees of solubility in different brands and grades of methanol, and the substances from different enterprises also have different degrees of solubility. Therefore, no information on the solubility of fudosteine in methanol is described in its monograph.<br>3.3 Effectively guarantee the drug safety and efficacy<br>3.3.1 Pay sustained attention to and strengthen the control of related substances. Deepen the qualitative and quantitative research on impurities, constantly optimize the separation methods and requirements for chromatographic system suitability of related substances, pay more attention to the source and classification of impurities, and scientifically and prudently compare, analyze and confirm them with the relevant methods in foreign pharmacopoeias, so as to scientifically and rigorously improve the determination methods of related substances. The test for related substances in more than 110 monographs has been added or revised in the Chinese Pharmacopoeia 2025 Edition (Volume II). An example is penicillamine. Penicillamine contains only one carbonyl unsaturated bond, and the others are single bonds, so it undergoes end absorption in the ultraviolet region, and some of its impurities have no ultraviolet absorption. Therefore, in the Pharmacopoeia 2020 Edition, detection at 210 nm can only control penicillamine disulfide, but not for other related substances. While only the penicillamine disulfide is controlled in the United States Pharmacopeia and European Pharmacopoeia. The HPLC-ELSD method (gradient elution) adopted in the new edition of pharmacopoeia improves the sensitivity and impurity testing ability of the method, and effectively controls penicillamine disulfide and other impurities. Another example is neomycin sulfate. A new HPLC-PAD method with four-potential detection waveform mode is used for the determination of related substances. With better separation and specificity, and ability to detect more impurities, this method solves the shortcomings of poor specificity and poor electrode durability of the three-potential waveform mode for the detection of related substances in the European Pharmacopoeia.<br>3.3.2 Strengthen the risk control of residual solvents and elemental impurities. Adhering to the national conditions of China, explore the control strategy of residual solvents and elemental impurities in marketed drugs by means of risk management concept and methods, and orderly promote the transformation and implementation of ICH guidelines in the Chinese Pharmacopoeia 2025 Edition (Volume II). The requirements of the transformation process are specified and explained in the General Notices as well as relevant general chapters and guidelines in Volume IV.<br>3.3.3 Strengthen the control of genotoxic impurities. In the process of development and revision of chemical drug standards, more and more efforts have been putting on the research and control of genotoxic impurities that may be introduced into drug production, such as (1) for ranitidine and its preparations, production requirements are added in the new edition of pharmacopoeia to draw attention to the potential for formationof genotoxic impurities; (2) for trimethoprim, aniline, which is genotoxic impurity, is used in the domestic synthesis process, so a GC method with good selectivity and high sensitivity using nitrogen phosphorus detector to determine its residue is prescribed in the new edition of pharmacopoeia with the same limit of 5ppm as that in the European Pharmacopoeia. Nitrogen phosphorus detector is commonly used for determination of aniline compounds. .<br>3.3.4 Strengthen the study on dissolution and release behavior of oral solid preparations and improve the test method for dissolution and release. Revise the test methods for dissolution and release as necessary according to the progress of equivalency evaluation. An example is Potassium Chloride Sustained-release Tablets. Based on the dissolution test method described in the specification of Potassium Chloride Sustained-release Tablets which passed the equivalency evaluation, dissolution test methods 3 to 6 are added in the new edition of pharmacopoeia standard. Another example is Leflunomide Tablets. The dissolution test method has been revised according to the equivalency evaluation results.<br>3.3.5 Optimize the infrared spectrum identification method, and enhance the scientific basis of the standard. On the basis of the spectra included in the previous Atlas of Infrared Spectra of Drugs, 92 new spectra have been added, 27 re-recorded, and the chemical structure formula, molecular formula and adopted names of some substances have been standardized, and compiled into the Atlas of Infrared Spectra of Drugs (2023 edition). The identification using infrared spectromphotometry described in related monographs in the Chinese Pharmacopoeia 2025 Edition (Volume II) has been revised.<br>3.4 Strengthen the applied research of new techniques and new methods to further ensure the quality control of drugs<br>3.4.1 Timely follow up the development of modern analytical techniques at home and abroad. Apply the nuclear magnetic resonance to the identification of the characteristics of low molecular heparin products, apply the mass spectrometry to the identification of the structure of some biochemical drugs and strengthen the use of physicochemical determination methods instead of biological determination methods, so as to make the specifications in line with international standards.<br>3.4.2 The research on the standard of the synthetic polypeptides with bivalirudin as representative is conducted in the new edition of pharmacopoeia to promote the improvement of the quality control level of such biochemical drugs. Compared with small molecule chemicals, the synthetic peptide drugs have more complex impurity profile, including impurities introduced from the starting material, process related impurities, degradation impurities, etc. In particular, peptide related impurities such as such as diastereomers, deletion, Insertion and substitution peptides may be produced during the synthesis process, which are difficult to separate and detect because of their very similar structure and chemical properties to the target products. In the process of standard development, it is clear that a total of 18 known impurities are included in the relevant registration standards of bivalirudin and the standards of the United States Pharmacopeia. After comparison and optimization of the methods in the above standards, two test methods for the related substances are established, and the 18 known impurities and other unknown impurities are studied. Reasonable and feasible test methods and limits are finally determined, which are basically consistent with the standards of bivalirudin and bivalirudin for injection included in the United States Pharmacopeia (official as of December 1, 2023), shortening the time lag in the implementation of standards.<br>3.4.3 In order to better meet the clinical needs, study on standard of drugs with clinical needs in shortage has been carried out, represented by high molecular weight substances in Posterior Pituitary Injection.<br>3.4.4 Track the progress of modern analytical techniques at home and abroad, and promote the application of mature analytical techniques. ？ Revise the medical oxygen content measurement item, take the lead in the online oxygen content determination by means of online detection technique, and revise the test method of impurities such as carbon monoxide and carbon dioxide from chemical precipitation to infrared analysis. The infrared analysis method is characterized by convenient operation, high sensitivity, online detection and other advantages, and conducive to the dynamic monitoring of product quality and process traceability. ？ Revise the related substance items and content determination item of penicillamine and change the test method for related substances from TLC to HPLC-ELSD, which improved the specificity and sensitivity of the method.<br>3.4.5 Carry out research to simplify test methods, which used to have excessively complicated pre-processing, to improve the operability of chemical drug standard tests. The vitamin D content determination calculation method in the new edition of pharmacopoeia eliminates the original correction factor determination step that requires to carry out concomitantly, and directly uses the correction factor F value to calculate the formula, avoiding the accidental error caused by concomitant determination, improving the accuracy of the determination, and reducing the operating steps, the analysis time, and the reagent consumption.<br>3.4.6 Standards of solutions for inhalation is included for the first time according to the new requirements. The terbutaline sulfate solution for inhalation is the first solution for inhalation to be included in the Chinese Pharmacopoeia according to the new requirements. In recent years, more and more preparations for inhsalation , especially solutions for inhalation, have been marketed in China and passed the equivalency evaluation. As a new way of drug therapy, the solutions for inhalation can directly act on the deposition sites such as the respiratory tract, avoiding the process of digestion of the drug through the gastrointestinal tract, so that the drug can take effect quickly, alleviating the adverse reaction of the drug in the body. The requirements of the Chinese Pharmacopoeia for the standard of such new drugs are also increasingly perfect, and inclusion of the standards of solution for inhalation that meet the requirements of the pharmacopoeia under the premise of safe and effective products and controllable quality can promote the high-quality development of new drug preparations and continuously meet for clinical needs for medications.<br>3.5 Strengthen the uniformity of drug standards<br>For the preparations having the same active ingredient, through thorough investigation and careful combing, and based on the research outcomes of domestic and foreign drug standards and find out the reasons for the differences in various standards, to ensure the consistency of the quality standards of such drugs, try to improve the reproducibility and accuracy of the method. For example, the assay method and method for related substances for inosine tablets, inosine capsules, inosine granules, inosine oral solution, inosine injection, inosine and glucose injection, inosine and sodium chloride injection, inosine for injection are unified.<br>3.6 Explore and study the existing test methods to form normative guidelines<br>For example, the compound amino acid injection is administered intravenously as nutritional support before and after surgery and for critically ill patients, and shall be used for a long time. Aluminum in compound amino acids and other parenteral injections will enter the blood directly without gastrointestinal digestion and absorption, while the aluminum cannot provide any therapeutic effect for patients, and has certain toxicity. The World Health Organization and the Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations (WHO/FAO) officially identified aluminum as a food contaminant which should be controlled in 1989. Both the United States Pharmacopeia (USP45) and the Japanese Pharmacopoeia (JP18) control the aluminum in parenteral nutrition preparations. The 9308 Guidelines for Determination of Aluminum Impurities in Compound Amino Acid Injections included in the Chinese Pharmacopoeia 2025 Edition can provide better protection of the medication safety for Chinese people.<br>4 Explore and innovate to ensure advancedness of chemical drug standards<br>4.1 Further expand the application of modern analytical techniques<br>Pay sustained attention to the suitability of column packing material for separation of drugs with different structures and polarities from impurities and make scientific classification of liquid chromatographic column packing material. Experiments have been carried out to find which chromatographic column is suitable. And then typical chromatograms, ultraviolet spectra and mass spectrum of related substances are collected to provide reliable supporting data information support for test and analysis of related substances using the pharmacopoeial standards. It has been confirmed by experiments that other brands of columns with the same packing material and size also can meet the requirements of system suitability described in 10 monographs in the Chinese Pharmacopoeia 2020 Edition (Volume II). Therefore, the informations of the column brands are deleted from the text of test for related substances in the above 10 monographss, further improving the universality of the pharmacopoeial method.<br>4.2 Continuously promote the application of green analysis technique<br>Revise the method described in 332 monographs involving highly toxic control substances in the Chinese Pharmacopoeia 2025 Edition (Volume II) and eliminate the relevant chemical reaction methods to minimize the use of highly toxic and reagents with high pollution, reduce carbon emissions and micro-environmental pollution, and reduce the damage to the health of laboratory personnel.<br>4.3 Further promote research on radiopharmaceutical test methods<br>In accordance with the composition and arrangement sequence of the individual monographs in the General Notices of the Chinese Pharmacopoeia 2025 Edition (Volume II), the chemical structure formula, molecular formula, molecular weight and CAS number of the active ingredients of 11 radioactive drugs with a definite structure have been added to meet the style requirments for monographas far as possibe. Meanwhile, drawing on the International Pharmacopoeia and the British Pharmacopoeia, the biological distribution determination of radiopharmaceuticals has been added to provide technical support for the detection of biological distribution of radiopharmaceuticals, filling the domestic gap.<br>5 Conclusion<br>During the development of the Chinese Pharmacopoeia 2025 Edition (Volume II), the ChPC has aways been based on the work concept of strengthening the foundation, improving the weak links, breaking the bottleneck, promoting improvement, and benchmarking against the international general rules to promote the construction and improvement of the government-led, enterprise-dominated and community-involved drug standard working mechanism, practice the concept of scientific supervision to further expand the application of mature analytical techniques, improve the specificity, accuracy and stability of test method, and strive to achieve high quality pharmacopoeial standards with comprehensive test items, applicable methods, and reasonable limits; thoroughly implement the new development concepts of innovation, coordination, green, openness, and sharing, give full play to the guiding role of the standard system in green testing, research and develop alternative methods to those using toxic control reagents to minimize or replace highly toxic and polluting test reagents, reduce carbon emissions and micro-environmental pollution, and fundamentally eliminate experimental safety hazards; promote the scientific transformation and implementation of related ICH technical guidelines, and strengthen the research on the risk control of related substances, residual solvents, elemental impurities, and genotoxic impurities; actively promote the improvement of chemical drug standards through multi-channels and make full use of the research outcomes of Sampling and Testing program and consistency evaluation for effective transformation into pharmacopoeia standards; further standardize the generic drug names, and help scientific drug administration, taking into account the drug attributes, technological innovation, process optimization, medical policy, clinical use, international harmonization, etc.<br>With the formal implementation of the Measures for the Administration of Drug Standards on January 1, 2024, we will further standardize the working requirements and working procedures at all stages of the establishment and maintenance of national chemical drug standards, and establish a scientific, practical and normative national chemical drug standard system with the Chinese Pharmacopoeia as the core, so as to promote the high-quality development of Chinas pharmaceutical industry, further ensure public drug safety, efficacy and accessibility and help China to realize the leap from a large pharmaceutical country to a pharmaceutical power as soon as possible.<br>　(Chinese Pharmacopoeia Commission) | 2025-07-14 |
