| Additions and Revisions of General Requirements of Physical and Chemical Analysis in the Chinese Pharmacopoeia 2025 Edition Volume IV | The General Requirements of Chinese Pharmacopoeia cover the common requirements of drug standards, serve as the basis of pharmacopoeia standards, and reflect the overall level of drug quality control in China and the competitive strength of China in the international drug trade. Among them, the general requirements of physical and chemical analysis include general identification tests, spectroscopy, chromatography, determination of physical constant, other determination methods, limit tests, tests for special properties or other indicators, molecular biological tests, special methods for traditional Chinese medicines and related guidelines, which are widely used in the quality control of traditional Chinese medicines, chemical drugs, biological products and pharmaceutical excipients. In recent years, new manufacturing methods continue to emerge, complex therapies and treatment methods continue to innovate, and the pharmaceutical industry is changing from localization to globalization. These changes in the development environment pose a number of challenges to the existing drug quality control and regulatory framework and affect various of methods and techniques used for drug quality control. In this context, the general requirements of physical and chemical analysis in the Chinese Pharmacopoeia 2025 Edition have been scientifically and systematically added and revised to meet the diversified needs of the current and future pharmaceutical industry; seize the development opportunity of China's pharmaceutical industry and focus on harmonization with the International Conference on Harmonization of Technical Requirements for Registration of Pharmaceuticals for Human Use (ICH) guidelines; give full play to the leading role of Chinese Pharmacopoeia, and learn from and adopt international advanced and mature analytical techniques to improve the drug quality control requirements, promote industry transformation and upgrading, and help the high-quality development of China's pharmaceutical industry. This article comprehensively introduces the main characteristics and content of the additions and revisions of the general requirements of physical and chemical analysis in the Chinese Pharmacopoeia 2025 Edition (Volume IV).<br>1 Promote the Transformation and Implementation of ICH Q Series Guidelines in Chinese Pharmacopoeia<br>1.1 Transformation and Implementation of ICH Q4 Pharmacopoeias Guidelines in Chinese Pharmacopoeia<br>On the basis of literature and industry research, method comparative study and validation, taking into account the interests appeal of domestic and foreign enterprises, the possible impact of the implementation of ICH Q4 on drug RD, production, testing, registration and supervision is evaluated. In addition, on the premise of ensuring suitability, 10 general requirements of physical and chemical analysis were added and revised in the Chinese Pharmacopoeia by direct harmonization and concurrent inclusion to be harmonized with ICH Q4, providing a more convenient new bridge for Chinas drugs to go international Direct harmonization is to revise or accept all requirements of Q4B on the basis of the original Chinese Pharmacopoeia, so that the relevant content of the Chinese Pharmacopoeia is harmonized with Q4B as far as possible, such as 0542 Capillary Electrophoresis, 0903 Test for Particulate Matter in Injections, 0923 Test for Tablet Friability, 0942 Minimum Fill, 0982 Determination of Particle Size and Particle Size Distribution (Method 2: Sieving) and 0993 Bulk Density and Tapped Density. Concurrent inclusion means that the current method included in the Chinese Pharmacopoeia is method 1 or determination method 1, and the ICH Q4B harmonized method is method 2 or determination method 2. Either method can be selected for the newly registered drugs and the used one should be indicated under the standard item. For the marketed drugs, the default method is method 1 before standard revisions or changes, or the ICH Q4B harmonized method can also be applied through the post-approval change procedure, such as 0841 Determination of Residue on Ignition, 0921 Determination of Disintegration (determination method), 0931 Dissolution and Drug Release Test (determination method) and 0940 Determination of Uniformity of Dosage Unit.<br>1.2 Transformation and Implementation of ICH Q2 Guidelines for Analytical Validation in Chinese Pharmacopoeia<br>With reference to ICH Q2 and Q14 and in combination with the actual work situation, the 9101 Guideline for Validation of Analytical Method, 9098 Guideline for Indeterminacy Evaluation, and 9097 Guideline for Interpretation and Processing of Analytical Data have been added and revised, and new concepts and tools for evaluation of analytical methods have been introduced to strengthen the guidance and validation of new analytical techniques, ensure that the analytical method can meet the desired application purpose, and improve the accuracy of product quality evaluation.<br>Revisions to 9101 Guideline for Validation of Analytical Method include: expand and clarify the scope of application of the guideline; modify the text structure framework, reclassify the performance characteristics to be validated, and revise the quality attributes (analysis items) to be measured and their corresponding performance characteristics; introduce concepts such as life cycle of analytical methods; clarify the status and role of robustness study and system suitability test; add suggestions for the selection of analytical method validation experiments and criteria; add the consideration of multivariate methods; expand the definition of scope by giving examples of the recommended reportable range of some analytical methods; add statistical considerations for method validation; introduce the joint validation of accuracy and precision; add terms and their definitions to enhance the understanding of analytical method validation; delete the description of the accuracy and precision requirements introduced by the HORWITZ equation.<br>The scientific interpretation and evaluation of analytical data is directly related to the scientificity and reliability of final decision of product quality. Add 9097 Guideline for Interpretation and Processing of Analytical Data to provide basic principles, together with data analysis and processing methods for statistics used in the Pharmacopoeia. It could guide the selection of scientific and reliable statistical methods for the pharmaceutical industry, present how to use statistical analysis methods to make standardized decisions on drug manufacturing processes and products, and provide a scientific and acceptable regulatory direction for using data to evaluate drug quality. The guideline mainly includes introduction, descriptive statistics, inferential statistics, basic statistical principles, common statistical methods, etc.<br>Measurement indeterminacy is used to characterize the dispersion reasonably assigned to the measured value. It is a non-negative parameter associated with the measured result, an assessment of the measured objective value within a certain value range, and a quantitative characterization of the quality of the measurement result. With the acceleration of the international integration process, the importance of measurement indeterminacy, as an indispensable part of evaluation of analytical methods and test results, has become increasingly prominent. <br>9098 Guideline for Indeterminacy Evaluation is added to provide guidance for the indeterminacy evaluation of drug testing laboratories, evaluate and show drug test results in a scientific and reasonable way, promote the wider application of indeterminacy evaluation methods in drug testing, and promote the continuous progressing of drug testing technology level. The guideline mainly includes: the significance and importance of measurement indeterminacy evaluation in the evaluation of drug test results; the suitability and main applications of indeterminacy evaluation in drug tests; scope; the basic method, basic step, calculation formula and result expression of indeterminacy evaluation.<br>1.3 Transformation and Implementation of ICH Q3C Guideline for Residual Solvents and Q3D Guideline for Elemental Impurities in Chinese Pharmacopoeia<br>General Chapter 0861 Determination of Residual Solvents is revised to be more harmonized with ICH Q3C. Based on the concept of risk assessment and in-process control, suggestions for evaluating and testing the solvents that are unavoidable in technology are put forward, and methods for evaluating and determining the residual solvents of drug substances, excipients and drug products are provided. General Chapter 0862 Elemental Impurity is added to be harmonized with ICH Q3D. The risk-based approach is adopted to evaluate elemental impurities and control elemental impurities existing in the full life cycle of drugs, which provides relevant basis for evaluating and controlling elemental impurities in drugs, as well as methods for verifying the types and limits of elemental impurities, and provides guidance for the selection, establishment, validation and use of determination methods for elemental impurities.<br>2 Add and Revise the Widely Used Instrumental Analysis Techniques to Promote the Innovation and Development of Domestic Pharmaceutical Analytical Instruments<br>Revise 0400 Optical Analysis Methods. Optical analysis methods are a category of analytical techniques widely used in drug quality control, including spectral and non-spectral methods. In recent years, some new optical technologies have been applied successively in drug standards as quality control methods, and have been included in the Chinese Pharmacopoeia as general methods or guidelines. Therefore, the name of original 0400 Spectrometry was revised to Optical Analysis Methods, adding light scattering method and other non-spectral methods, and classifying various optical methods.<br>Add 9094 Guideline for Analytical Instrument Qualification. The analytical instrument qualification is an important part of the management of analytical instruments throughout the life cycle, aiming to ensure that the instrument performance is reliable and the data obtained from measurement is accurate and constantly meets the intended use. The guideline is designed to establish a unified technical guideline to provide guidance on how to conduct analytical instrument qualification in drug quality research, drug production quality control and drug testing, and provide reference for the classification of analytical instruments, determination of the confirmation scope and specific implementation of various stages of instrument confirmation so as to ensure the standardized management of laboratory analytical instruments throughout the life cycle, and to promote laboratory quality management in line with international standards. The guideline mainly includes introduction, data quality composition, analytical instrument qualification process, implementation of analytical instrument qualification, software validation, change control and analytical instrument qualification documents.<br>Add 9032 Guideline for Weighing with Analytical Electronic Balance. is one of the most common operations in drug analysis experiments, and the basis and core to ensure the accuracy and reliability of analysis results. The guideline is added to be in line with the world's advanced technology requirements and in line with the current national conditions of China to make the technical requirements easy to operate. The guideline mainly includes instrument performance qualification, balance installation, use and maintenance, weighing specifications and other operations.<br>Add 9031 Guideline for Chemical Imaging. Chemical imaging can provide both the composition and spatial information of a sample, visually analyze the distribution characteristics of the sample surface, and realize the rapid and non-destructive comparison between different samples. It is an important supplement to the traditional spectral analysis methods. The additions and revisions of the guideline aims to provide guidance for chemical imaging in drug composition identification, content distribution assessment, physical morphology characterization and other applications, and promote the application of multi-source information fusion detection technology in China's pharmaceutical industry. The guideline mainly includes scope, imaging characteristics, imaging system, acquisition mode and imaging process.<br>Add 0408 Atomic Fluorescence Spectrometry. To promote the application of scientific instruments independently developed in China's pharmaceutical industry, the guideline is suitable for the limit test and assay of some heavy metal elements in Chinese medicinal materials, Chinese patent medicines, chemicals and excipients, mainly including the general requirements, interference and calibration for instruments, preparation, procedures, detection limits and quantitative limits of the test solution.<br>Add and revise 0471 Scanning Electron Microscopy. The scanning electron microscope can not only observe the surface morphology of the sample, but also analyze the composition, elements and crystallography. The scanning electron microscope has a broad application prospect in the testing of traditional Chinese medicine, chemical drugs, biological products, pharmaceutical excipients and pharmaceutical packaging materials, etc. The General Chapter describes the instrument type, detection principle, scope, instruments and detection method, etc. to expand its further application in the field of drug research and development and drug quality control.<br>Revise 0402 Infrared Spectrometry. The main revisions include: revise the name of the General Chapter and adjust the structure of the General Chapter; add and revise the scope and spectrum representation unit; in the measurement mode section, supplement the principle and add diffuse reflection and infrared microscope content; in the instrument section, put forward the requirements for instrument calibration and system suitability protocol; simply summarize the qualitative and quantitative methods, add and revise the spectrogram comparison and result interpretation methods, supplement the specific method of quantitative analysis and give the method validation protocol, etc.<br>Delete 9104 Guideline for Near-Infrared (NIR) Spectrophotometry and add as 0403 Near-infrared (NIR) Spectrometry to promote the further application of NIR Spectrometry. The main revisions include: (1) Revise the scope and suitability of the method; (2) Revise the measurement mode, use the transreflection mode as the third measurement mode, alongside the transmission and reflection modes, and add the introduction of the sample measuring device; (3) Revise the detailed methods and general requirements of instrument calibration and self-test; (4) Revise the content of qualitative and quantitative methods, divide the methods into three categories according to the application: qualitative, limit and trend analysis, and quantitative analysis, and simplify the description of specific methods and processes by referring to the 9096 Guideline for Chemometrics, so as to make the content more compact; (5) Add the key technical requirements for the application of NIR Spectrometry.<br>Revise 0431 Mass Spectrometry. According to the application practice and the development of mass spectrometry techniques in recent years, the content such as mature ion source, mass analyzer, fragmentation mode, data acquisition mode, instrument qualification, method validation and verification of the current mass spectrometry has been added.<br>Revise 0441 Nuclear Magnetic Resonance Spectrometry. To improve the advancement of the method and its guidance for practical application, the content of instrument qualification, method verification and validation, two-dimensional nuclear magnetic resonance and solid state nuclear magnetic resonance, etc. has been emphasized and supplemented and the harmonization and consistency with the existing guidelines in the Chinese Pharmacopoeia has been enhanced.<br>Revise 0461 X-ray Fluorescence Spectrometry. The main revisions include supplementing and improving the introduction, sample preparation and quantitative determination method and adding and revising the content related to accuracy, repeatability, intermediate precision, specificity, limit of quantification, linearity, range, robustness and qualification in the method validation and verification.<br>Revise 0502 Thin-Layer Chromatography. The resolution of quantitative thin-layer chromatography is calculated based on the distance between two adjacent peaks (spots) and the peak width of two adjacent peaks. In this revision, a formula was added to calculate the resolution with peak width at half height. However, considering the continuity of each edition of the Chinese Pharmacopoeia, the original formula is still retained as a transition, and it is cleared that in case of an objection, the resolution shall be subject to the calculation result of peak width at half height. In addition, the content of thin-layer chromatography conditions (parameters) allowed to be adjusted was added, illustrated by simple examples.<br>Revise 0512 High Performance Liquid Chromatography, improve the adjustment range of chromatographic parameters, add the description of the influence of retention volume on gradient separation, add the description of solution preparation, revise and supplement the system suitability test and add the standard curve determination method in procedures.<br>Revise 0513 Ion Chromatography. Add the main composition of ion chromatograph; recommend the reference proportion range of organic solvents in the eluent, and supplement the advantages and disadvantages of adding organic solvents; add the precautions for the preparation of eluent and the description of the electrolytic eluent online generator; describe in detail the conductivity detector, ampere detector and mass spectrometry detector commonly used in ion chromatography; clarify the purpose of sample pretreatment, revise the pretreatment method, and add valve switching on-line matrix elimination method and combustion method.<br>Revise 0514 Size-Exclusion Chromatography. The main revisions include supplementing the instrument composition required for size exclusion chromatography, and adding common detectors; replacing the resolution with peak-to-valley ratio <br>; revising the determination method of molecular weight and molecular weight distribution of biomacromolecular polymer to gel permeation chromatography; adding size exclusion chromatography-static light scattering method.<br>Revise 0521 Gas Chromatography. The range of chromatographic parameters allowed to be adjusted has been specified in order to improve the operability of gas chromatography. The main revisions include: adding an item of chromatographic parameter adjustment, and adding and revising a table of allowable adjustment range of chromatographic parameters; defining related requirements such as peaking sequence and detection limit after parameter adjustment, and verifying the method if necessary; defining the treatment rules in case of objection to the determination results after adjusting the chromatographic conditions; specifying the requirements for designated or recommended column brands under monographs.<br>3 Add and Revise Standards for Analytical Methods Related to Drug Efficacy, Safety and Quality Controllability<br>Add Method 8 Reciprocating holder method and Method 9 Diffusion cell method in 0931 Dissolution and Drug Release Test for the formulation process development and quality control of transdermal patches, sustained-release preparations, semi-solid preparations for topical use, etc. to provide alternative new methods for the quality evaluation of related specific dosage forms.<br>Add 0953 Determination of Acid-neutralizing Capacity. The acid-neutralizing capacity test is to test the critical quality attributes of antacids. Establish the general requirements of acid-neutralizing capacity determination and standardize the instruments, parameters, operating conditions and process of acid-neutralizing capacity determination, so as to make the instrument and operation more standardized, mainly including five parts: instruments, test preparation, procedures (including Method 1 Single-point method and Method 2 Multi-point method), interpretation of results and notes. In this method, the unique small glass method device of Chinese Pharmacopoeia is used as the test device for the acid-neutralizing capacity determination, which is conducive to the popularization and application in China.<br>Add 0961 Dynamic Vapor Sorption. Dynamic vapor sorption (DVS) is a method to study the interaction between substances and vapor, using the relative humidity, mass and (or) time relationship to accurately measure the amount of solvent vapor adsorbed by substances, and evaluate the trend and degree of sorption or absorption of solvent vapor. The DVS is used to investigate the hygroscopicity, crystal form, amorphous content, phase transformation, stability and water activity, etc. of the drug and determine the packaging and storage conditions of a drug and screen the prescription of the preparation. It can also be used in conjunction with spectral technology to obtain spectral information of the drug. The method mainly includes five parts: instruments, instrument qualification, procedures, result analysis and notes.<br>Revise 9103 Guideline for Hygroscopicity. The hygroscopicity of drugs is an important characteristic that affects the drug stability, efficacy and safety. In 9103 Guideline for Hygroscopicity, Method 2 Dynamic vapor sorption has been added to determine the hygroscopicity using the instruments in 0961 Dynamic Vapor Sorption. Compared with Method 1, this method reduces the interference of external environment, saves the manpower, shortens the measurement time and can continuously monitor the hygroscopicity degree of samples under different humidity.<br>Add Method 3 Dynamic light scattering and Method 4 Light obscuration in 0982 Determination of Particle Size and Particle Size Distribution. Two methods have been used to determine the particle size distribution in the standards of monographs propofol emulsion injection and fat emulsion injection (C14-24) in Chinese Pharmacopoeia. The above two methods have been added and revised to further meet the needs of quality control of related monographs.<br>Add 9022 Guideline for Intrinsic Dissolution Determination. Intrinsic dissolution rate refers to the dissolution rate of the tablet of pure solid substance under constant surface area, expressed by the amount of dissolved substance per unit time and unit area, which can dynamically reflect the dissolution behavior of drugs in the dissolution medium. It can not only reflect the dissolution rate, but also reflect the information of traditional solubility under certain conditions. It can also be used to determine the solubility of drugs in the biological agent classification system, provide a reference for the selection of drug dosage forms and prescription design, and be used as a means of drug quality control for the control of drug production process. The guideline mainly includes overview, instruments, procedures, data analysis and evaluation of results.<br>Add 9023 Guideline for Solubility Measurement. Solubility is a physical property of a drug that reflects the ability of the solvent to dissolve the solute. Accurate measurement of drug solubility is important to understand the quality control and drug delivery of pharmaceutical preparations. The guideline provides a scientific, reasonable and reliable guide for solubility measurement, mainly including the definition of dissolution and solubility, different expression methods and units of solubility, and the meaning of accurate measurement of solubility; thermodynamic equilibrium and solubility; solubility estimation method in water; factors affecting solubility and solubility measurement; solubility measurement methods.<br>Add 9024 Guideline for Proteomic Analytical Methods and Applications. Proteomic analytical method is to quickly and efficiently detect, identify and quantify basic information such as composition, quantity and interaction in protein samples through a variety of analytical techniques, mainly mass spectrometry, or high-throughput analytical methods based on two-dimensional electrophoresis, X-ray analysis, nuclear magnetic resonance spectroscopy, transmission electron microscopy and bioinformatics principles, and then reveal the biological characteristics such as protein function and regulation in the living body. The guideline applies to the analytical study of proteomics in the aspects of protein composition and change rules, protein post-translational modification and protein-protein interaction, standardizes the establishment of proteomic analytical methods, quality control of analysis process and data analysis, and ensures the repeatability and reliability of proteomic analysis results. The guideline mainly includes scope, analysis strategies, analytical methods, analysis quality control and application.<br>Revise 9303 Guideline for Determination of Pigments. Add provisions and methods for the legal use of pigments, and further clearly define the application scenarios of the guideline; form method establishment strategies for special cases such as excipients, preparations and possible illegal dyeing in the basic principles and add related content of inorganic pigment test; in the basic content, add the liquid-phase high resolution mass spectrometry and combine with the similar content in liquid-phase triple quadrupole mass spectrometry into the liquid chromatography-mass spectrometry method.<br>Revise 9305 Guideline for Determination of Mycotoxins in Traditional Chinese Medicine. (1) Add and revise types of mycotoxins to cover the types of mycotoxins with strong toxicity, high pollution rate and high concern produced by the major five toxigenic bacteria genera, and introduce the concept of cryptic mycotoxins. (2) Systematically introduce a variety of detection methods for mycotoxins, and describe in detail the application strategies of each method according to the characteristics of each method, the requirements and application characteristics of practical testing. (3) Add and revise two new methods for quantitative determination: citrinin high performance liquid chromatography and liquid chromatography-tandem mass spectrometry. Establish general sample pretreatment method and chromatography-mass spectrometry conditions. (4) Add and revise a detection method for high throughput and rapid sieving of 33 mycotoxins simultaneously using high performance liquid chromatography-triple quadrupole mass spectrometry, and establish the extraction and purification pretreatment technology and chromatography-mass spectrometry conditions.<br>Add 9120 Guideline for Amino Acid Analysis. Amino acid analysis method shall be used to determine the amino acid content or amino acid composition of compound amino acid injection, polypeptide drugs and traditional Chinese medicine monographs included in Chinese Pharmacopoeia. The guideline guides the selection of appropriate amino acid analysis methods during the implementation of the pharmacopoeia standards, mainly including basic requirements, hydrolysis of protein and peptide samples, amino acid determination and data processing.<br>4 Include More Scientific, Objective and Simple Analytical Detection Techniques and Promote the Inclusion of Green Analytical Chemistry Techniques<br>By adding and revising the Method 3 atomic fluorescence spectrometry, 0822 Limit Test for Arsenic is gradually transformed from non-quantitative method to quantitative method, from traditional chemical test method to modern instrument analysis. This method is divided into three parts, including the determination conditions, the preparation of standard arsenic solution and test solution, and procedures. And other issues needing attention are put forward in the notes. In addition, to solve the problem that arsenic trioxide reagent is highly toxic and difficult to buy, the method of preparing standard arsenic solution from arsenic single element standard solution has been added. The standard value method is added to Method 3 Colour difference metre method in 0901 Colour of Solution to promote the popularization and application of objective, accurate and digital test methods. The safe, non-toxic and stable polymer microsphere turbidity standard solution has been added to 0902 Clarity of Solution, as a supplement to the potentially carcinogenic formazine turbidity reference suspension.<br>5 Promote the Application of PAT in Chinese Pharmacopoeia<br>The description of the specific requirements as an effective tool for process analytical technology and their applications has been added and revised to 0402 Infrared Spectrometry and 0403 Near-infrared (NIR) Spectrometry. 9095 Guideline for Multivariate Statistical Process Control and 9096 Guideline for Chemometrics have been added and revised to introduce the types of models used in the process analytical technology (PAT) development, and the commonly used data processing methods in the process of model establishment, etc. so as to further promote the application of process analytical technology in China's pharmaceutical industry.<br>6 Improve Normative Expression, Strengthen the Formulation of Suitability Requirements, and Ensure the Reliability of Analysis Results<br>Revise 0631 Determination of pH Value. The scope of application in 2. Notes (6) has been revised from a liquid with weak buffering capacity or a liquid without a buffer effect to low ionic strength solutions. According to the pH meters on the market and in the laboratory, the calibration and determination methods in the original 2. Notes (6) have been revised. Two methods are provided to improve the pH determination of low ionic strength solutions: (1) Increase the electrode equilibrium time; (2) Add saturated potassium chloride solution as an ionic strength improver to increase the ionic strength of the test solution and reduce the interference of streaming potential and liquid junction potential on pH measurement.<br>The reagents, test solutions, test papers, buffer solutions, indicator solutions and volumetric solutions used in drug inspection and testing play an important role in identification, tests, assay and other items, and their quality directly affects the quality of drug analysis and testing results. Revise the general requirements, critical quality indexes of commonly used reagents and reagent monographs in 8001 Reagents. Add and revise definitions and 5 test solutions including ammonium molybdate TS in 8002 Test Solutions. Add and revise definitions in 8003 Test Papers. Add and revise definitions, purpose and composition as well as Phosphate BS (pH 6.0) in 8004 Buffer Solutions. Add and revise the overall requirements and revise the preparation methods of some indicator solutions in 8005 Indicator Solutions. Add and revise definitions and preparation, calibration storage requirements and ethanolic sodium hydroxide VS, etc. in 8006 Volumetric Solutions.<br>Table of Standard Atomic Weights is the basis of molecular weight calculation of compounds and the basis for accurate expression of relative mass of compounds. According to the latest edition of Table of Standard Atomic Weights published by the International Union of Pure and Applied Chemistry (IUPAC), the revisions include the inclusion and classification of all the elements in the periodic table of elements, explanation of the numbers in atomic weight parentheses and brackets, and addition of remarks.<br>7 Brief Summary<br>The General Requirements of the Chinese Pharmacopoeia Volume IV are an important basis to ensure the correct implementation of national drug standards. The general requirements of physical and chemical analysis in the Chinese Pharmacopoeia 2025 Edition Volume IV are more harmonized with the ICH guidelines on the basis of ensuring suitability, and provide a more convenient new bridge for Chinas drugs to go international; absorb new methods, new technologies, new equipment and new ideas developed in recent years, promote scientific and technological innovation in the pharmaceutical industry, and enhance the international competitiveness of Chinese pharmaceutical enterprises; strengthen the formulation of safety and efficacy analytical methods and quality control requirements to provide standardized testing technology support for achieving full process quality control, and better meet the needs of drug research and development, production, quality control and supervision in China.<br>　(Chinese Pharmacopoeia Commission) | 2025-07-01 |
